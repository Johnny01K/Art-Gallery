About

Hello! I am Ninjuubi from Serbia and I started my art journey about 5 years ago when I was bored and picked up a pen and paper. My first drawings were uploaded on Instagram in 2018. and since then I mostly upload artworks weekly. I am self tought and do graphite art, prismacolors (colored) art and also digitally enhanced art through a phone app. In art gallery, you can check almost all my work and upcoming drawings that will also be published here.

Materials are:
